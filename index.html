About Us

Created in 1922 by the Wild family, the Wild Circus is internationally recognized.

He makes young and old children dream thanks to his wonderful show.

Take a magical break when entering the Wild Circus show, and discover these acrobats, these animal trainers and his clowns.

Small detail, shoes must be placed at the entrance of the tent: it is the family tradition.
